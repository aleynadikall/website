ILYA REPIN

Ilya Repin draws his subjects and imagination from contemporary reality; She focuses on the main problems of Russian life. Dmitri Sarabianov states that Repin is directly interested in human relations; He argues that his aim is to portray the world in all its complexity and contradictions. This is a correct determination, as Repin, while characterizing human figures and portraits, reflects spiritual movements that are difficult to notice at first glance, penetrating even the smallest details.
