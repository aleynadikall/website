GUSTAV KLIMT

Gustav Klimt generally included women in his works. It is known that Klimt likes to be with women in his daily life and is inspired by women. In his works, he mostly dealt with the deep feelings of women, fertility, sexuality, love and eroticism, death and life cycle about life. Instead of telling about himself and his art, Klimt advised those who want to understand him and his art to look carefully at his works and try to understand who he is and what he wants to do in his works.
