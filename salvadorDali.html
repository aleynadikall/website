SALVADOR DALI

According to some, he is one of the greatest artists the modern world has known, according to others, he is just a madman with an extraordinary mustache. He is crazy who does not hesitate to describe himself as follows: “There are two great painters in the world, one is Pablo and the other is me, but I am bigger.” Dali, who was constantly on the agenda not only with his art but also with his lifestyle during his lifetime, still retains its place in our memories today, as well as his mustache.
