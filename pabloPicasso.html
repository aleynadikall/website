PABLO PICASSO

There are some masters in the history of art that even if you see hundreds of their works, every new exhibition opened in their name makes you want to see and savor as soon as possible. Pablo Picasso is one of these masters for me. “I didn't paint the war because I'm not someone who portrays things like a photographer. But I have no doubt that war is in all the paintings I make. Perhaps later historians will discover this and show how my style has changed under the influence of the war.” This is how the painter Pablo Picasso told a war correspondent in Paris, days after his liberation from the Nazi occupation, how the war years, which covered the long period of his life, affected him.
